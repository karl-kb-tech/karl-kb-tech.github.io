What is Emotional Intelligence?
Emotional intelligence or quotient is a person's ability to identify, control, express and evaluate their own emotions or emotional state; it is also their ability to identify the same in others, and then how you can them feel.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ or intelligence quotient is the total measure of someone's intelligence, measured across a wide range of topic from science, maths, biology etc… Having a high IQ does not necessarily correlate with having a high EQ, although both can be taught and increased

Why is it important?

I am guessing “it” in the instance would be EQ, but could be IQ? Having a high EQ will help you relate a lot better with others in both your professional career and personal life. EQ maybe what gets you hired over the person with a higher IQ who is competing for the same job. But IQ is also important, no one is going to hire someone that has no idea what they doing for the role - no matter how relatable the person is.
